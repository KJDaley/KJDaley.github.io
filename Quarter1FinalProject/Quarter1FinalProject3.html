My Online Gamer Presence

CowCraft was the first Minecraft server that I regular played on. It wasn't a bad community, but it was starting to die down when I really started getting into it. I have fond memories from this time, lots of fun projects, funny moments with friends, and some really engaging conversations. Though a lot of the people I met here I no longer communicate with, due to problems that have occured, I did enjoy the time I had spent here.

BlockBit stated of as a replacement for the CowCraft community I had lost, but it quickly became something more. I pulled the people from my old communities, along with new people I met along the way, into a new group, one that would be much more friendly to everyone. We played Minecraft, Team Fortress 2, and many other games together, as well as discuss a variety of topics. I still do regularly chat with them.

Super Smash Bros Club is a student-run club here at SLUH, where fans of the series come together to play each other. When I first heard of the club in Freshman year, I was skeptical of joining, but after discussing with my counsler about my inablity to communicate, she suggested this club as a way to open myself up. As the self-proclaimed third-in-command of the club, I do enjoy myself here. We continue to grow and expand as a club.
